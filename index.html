Welcome to
Physiolight Infinity
Physio Rehab Centre

Physiolight Infinity is a unique Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinic in the lap of Dhauladhars, Dharamshala, focused on treating patients with a new, innovative, yet more advanced and accurate approach to bring forth an intense, long lasting change in the condition of patients visiting us, and helping them restore, maintain, and make the most of their mobility, function, and well being.

We treat conditions ranging from: NEURO (Spinal cord injury, Stroke, Traumatic brain injury, Parkinson's, Alzheimer, Diabetic Neuropathy, Bell's Palsy, and other Nerve Compressions etc. )

ORTHO (Osteoarthritis, PIVD, Disc degeneration, Cervical Spondylosis, Frozen shoulder, Tennis elbow, Plantar Fasciitis, Post fracture stiffness, LBA etc.)

PEDIATRICS (Cerebral palsy, Autism, Down's syndrome, Gross motor delay, Spina Bifida, Muscular Dystrophy, Weight Challenges etc.)

SPORTS (Ankle Sprain, Hamstring Strain, Knee injuries like ACL tear, Groin Strain, Shoulder injuries, Stress fractures, CTS, Achilles injury, Neck injury, etc.)
