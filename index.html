Welcome to
Physiolight Infinity
Physio Rehab Centre

Physiolight Infinity is a unique Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinic in the lap of Dhauladhars, Dharamshala, focused on treating patients with a new, innovative, yet more advanced and accurate approach to bring forth an intense, long lasting change in the condition of patients visiting us, and helping them restore, maintain, and make the most of their mobility, function, and well being.

Physiotherapy is not as prevalent as other medical fields in our country, and especially in our state Himachal, but we here at Physiolight Infinity hope to change that by making patients more aware and conscious of the benefits of accurate, patient oriented physiotherapy sessions. Patients suffering from almost any disorder can benefit from our treatment, and break down the barriers of pain hindering them from going on smoothly with their lives, hence further elaborating our tagline, Breaking All Barriers.

Our Services

NEURO
Spinal cord injury, Stroke, Traumatic brain injury, Parkinson's, Alzheimer, Diabetic Neuropathy, Bell's Palsy, and other Nerve Compressions etc.

ORTHO
Osteoarthritis, PIVD, Disc degeneration, Cervical Spondylosis, Frozen shoulder, Tennis elbow, Plantar Fasciitis, Post fracture stiffness, LBA etc.

PEDIATRICS
Cerebral palsy, Autism, Down's syndrome, Gross motor delay, Spina Bifida, Muscular Dystrophy, Weight Challenges etc.

SPORTS
Ankle Sprain, Hamstring Strain, Knee injuries like ACL tear, Groin Strain, Shoulder injuries, Stress fractures, CTS, Achilles injury, Neck injury, etc.

About him

Dr. Nischay Paul Thakur, after graduating from Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, worked with the Honorable Governor of H.P, Bandaru Dattatreya, and Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur in Shimla, H.P.

Thereafter Dr. Nischay worked with different Advance Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinics like Mission Walk in Hyderabad, Spinofit plus in Pune, and Aadhya healthcare in Delhi.

After learning the core of rehabilitation he planned on starting his own venture in his home state Himachal as there was a lack of Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinics in a state where it was really needed, as statistics clearly indicated that people in Himachal suffer a lot from Orthopaedic issues, due to the rough terrain of the province.

He's full of enthusiasm and extremely thankful to be able to provide his advanced approach to his patients, and bring about a change in people's perception of physiotherapy.
