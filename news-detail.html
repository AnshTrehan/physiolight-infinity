First visit

How to prepare for the first visit?
If you've never been to a physiotherapist before, without objection, there must be a first day
The first visit is extremely important as it is the moment you come in and explain

We really want the patient to feel at ease, so we encourage you to do proper preparation for the visit. It will certainly facilitate the diagnostic process.

What should you take with you?

  • Medical documentation (e.g. X-ray, ultrasound, magnetic resonance imaging, tomography hospital discharge reports etc.)
  • sports outfit that will allow freedom during the examination (it can be a T-shirt, shorts, tracksuit)
  • changeable footwear (sports, flip-flops)

About the author

Dr. Nischay Paul Thakur, after graduating from Lovely Professional University, Jalandhar, worked with the Honorable Governor of H.P, Bandaru Dattatreya, and Chief Minister Jai Ram Thakur in Shimla, H.P.

Thereafter Dr. Nischay worked with different Advance Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinics like Mission Walk in Hyderabad, Spinofit plus in Pune, and Aadhya healthcare in Delhi.

After learning the core of rehabilitation he planned on starting his own venture in his home state Himachal as there was a lack of Physiotherapy Rehabilitation Clinics in a state where it was really needed, as statistics clearly indicated that people in Himachal suffer a lot from Orthopaedic issues, due to the rough terrain of the province.

He's full of enthusiasm and extremely thankful to be able to provide his advanced approach to his patients, and bring about a change in people's perception of physiotherapy.
